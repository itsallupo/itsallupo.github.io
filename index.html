About Me

My name is Jason Gregory Maddle. I am 28 years old and I am a full-stack web developer. I've loved computers for as long as I can remember and HTML/CSS was one of my very first steps into coding when I was about twelve. I am mostly self taught, though recently I have been working my way through the freecodecamp.com full-stack web dev course so that I can actually have a certification in this field since this is what I wan't to do. I currently work managing a small website but my job has more to with server admin than any actual coding. Don't get me wrong, I love my job and I consider myself lucky to be able to be doing this, but at some point I would like to do something that is more on the coding end of the spectrum than on the administration end.
